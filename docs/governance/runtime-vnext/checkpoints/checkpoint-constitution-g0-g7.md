# Checkpoint Constitution (G0-G7)

## G0 Doctrine Lock
- Purpose: ratify constitutional runtime and authority model.
- Entry: governance charter drafted.
- Evidence: charter, authority matrix, protected-surface registry.
- Pass: charter signed and active.
- Fail: unresolved authority or scope.

## G1 Dependency Certification
- Purpose: prevent hidden dependency drift.
- Entry: G0 pass.
- Evidence: dependency graph, lineage map, protected-path map.
- Pass: critical dependencies certified.
- Fail: unknown dependency affecting protected path.

## G2 Language Compliance
- Purpose: block score-first and generic-language drift.
- Entry: G1 pass.
- Evidence: language scan, approved vocabulary conformance report.
- Pass: no prohibited primary UX terms in governed surfaces.
- Fail: score-first regression or contamination.

## G3 Ontology Conformance
- Purpose: preserve CBIO/CCSIM continuity.
- Entry: G2 pass.
- Evidence: ontology mapping, transition parity matrix.
- Pass: no flattening or transition collapse.
- Fail: ontology mismatch or transition loss.

## G4 Explainability Continuity
- Purpose: preserve evidence-to-guidance traceability.
- Entry: G3 pass.
- Evidence: explainability trace maps, deterministic attribution report.
- Pass: provenance and rationale continuity intact.
- Fail: evidence/rationale disconnect.

## G5 Manager Interpretation Stability
- Purpose: preserve manager intervention semantics and psych safety.
- Entry: G4 pass.
- Evidence: manager impact simulation, psych safety audit.
- Pass: no punitive/ranking drift; intervention continuity retained.
- Fail: manager semantic drift.

## G6 Protected-Surface Readiness
- Purpose: verify protected-surface prerequisites before any pilot consideration.
- Entry: G5 pass and full certification pack.
- Evidence: protected-path certification, rollback drill, parity dossier.
- Pass: exhaustive continuity certifications complete.
- Fail: any protected-path uncertainty.

## G7 Controlled Pilot Approval / NO-GO
- Purpose: final constitutional authorization.
- Entry: G6 pass.
- Evidence: pilot dossier, kill-switch proof, telemetry plan.
- Pass: constrained pilot approved.
- Fail: missing containment, missing parity, or unresolved Sev1 risk.

## Severity Handling
- Sev1: immediate freeze, rollback prep, constitutional incident review.
- Sev2: gated freeze and corrective review.
- Sev3: remediation cycle.

## Rollback Requirement (All Gates)
Any fail at G3+ requires rollback viability proof and re-validation before re-entry.
