# Governance Charter

## Scope
This charter governs migration sequencing, protected intelligence preservation, checkpoint authority, escalation, and pilot authorization.

## Constitutional Authorities
- Governance Council
- Ontology Governance Owner
- Deterministic Systems Owner
- Explainability Lead
- Manager Intelligence Lead
- AI Behavior Governance Lead
- Product Governance Authority
- Migration Governance Authority

## Veto and Quorum
- Sev1 domains require quorum: Governance Council + affected domain owners.
- Veto holders for critical integrity: Ontology Owner, Deterministic Owner, Explainability Lead, Governance Council Chair.
- No unilateral override for Sev1 failures.

## Freeze Rule
Any protected-path uncertainty triggers constitutional freeze and escalation.

## Audit Rule
All checkpoint decisions require persisted evidence and decision logs.
