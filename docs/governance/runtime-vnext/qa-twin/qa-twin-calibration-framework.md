# QA Twin Calibration Framework

## Calibration Objective
Calibrate QA Twin to constitutional upstream authority:
- HCP Predictive Builder + Adaptive RPS authority model
- deterministic continuity
- ontology continuity
- explainability continuity
- protected-path integrity
- manager interpretation stability
- AI Coach downstream-governed role

## QA Twin Must Validate
1. deterministic parity
2. CBIO/CCSIM continuity and transition preservation
3. evidence provenance and rationale lineage
4. AI Coach non-inversion (downstream role only)
5. manager interpretation continuity
6. absence of score-first regression
7. semantic drift and simplification drift

## QA Twin Must Detect
- architectural inversion
- ontology relocation to generic prompts
- unconstrained orchestration replacement
- transition flattening
- evidence/rationale disconnect
- shared-service semantic drift

## Failure Policy
Any protected-path uncertainty => FAIL => Governance Freeze + Constitutional Escalation.
